About the Family

UT Saaya was established in 2008 as one of the first Bollywood Fusion teams at UT. We have grown to become a nationally competing dance team that performs styles such as hiphop, bollywood, bharatnatyam, contemporary, kathak, kuthu, and bhangra.

Family

Our family culture emphasizes fun along with winning, alumni support, team formals at the end of the year, independence to showcase your skills, highest retention rate, performance of all styles. We emphasize our culture so that we can be proud of the the team we are a part of

Competitive

We travel across the state and country to compete in the DDN circuit which includes Bollywood America, Legends, and local competitions. We also strongly believe in dancing with a purpose by trying to address issues we as a team are passionate about through our sets.

Growth

Our goal is to help each individual dancer become the best they can be in all the variety of styles we perform in. Many members come in with little to no training, and after one year, they are confident in styles they would have never thought of trying.
